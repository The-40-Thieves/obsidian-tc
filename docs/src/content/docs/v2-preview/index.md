---
title: V2 Preview
description: A look ahead — capabilities scoped to V2, not part of the v1.0 surface.
---

v1.0 ships the complete G2.1 tool surface and the M7 hardening gate. A few
capabilities are deliberately **out of scope for v1.0** and reserved for V2:

- **Clustering & advanced retrieval** — the native module carries V2 clustering
  hooks, but the clustering tools themselves are not exposed in v1.0.
- **Richer hybrid ranking** beyond the v1.0 BM25 + vector blend.

These are previews of direction, not commitments of schedule. The v1.0 tool surface
is stable; V2 capabilities will be added additively.
